Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 500g
Rice 400g
Ketchup 4 tablespoons
Salt 1 teaspoon
Soy sauce 4 tablespoons
Sugar 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Rinse the rice with water and cook it.
Step 2: Cut the chicken and fry it for 15 minutes.
Step 3: Mix the rest of the ingredients and boil them with the chicken.
Step 4: Serve and eat. Priyatnogo appetita!
